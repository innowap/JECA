Jikeme Emmanuel Cinematography Academy

The Mount Zion Institute of Christian Drama unifies and spiritually empowers drama ministers in other nations they go, by helping the drama ministers, after training, to stay united informing an association with same visions and goals in their various churches and cities.

Talking about the venue of this Institution, the Institute took off on July 5th 1991 at the then Mount Zion Faith Ministries Int'l secretariat, Aserifa-Ola Layout, Ile-Ife, Nigeria. The Pioneer Students of the MZI actually used to sleep on mats whenever they came for the courses throughout the 12 Weekends. Later on, various venues were used for the Courses: (Camp Young, Ede, Osun State, Nigeria; Trans Nigeria Motel, Ile-Ife, Osun State, Nigeria; Deborah Missions Retreat Centre, Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria) until November 2002, when the Institute moved to its permanent site at Habitation of Faith (The Mount Zion Training and Retreat Centre), located at Ile-Ife, Osun State, Nigeria.

Up to 2,500 students have graduated from the Mount Zion Institute of Christian Drama courses held in, Nigeria; London, UK; Arlington, Dallas; Winnipeg, Canada. In Nigeria, the MZICD has impacted in the lives of many Drama Ministers, and the traces of its blessing can be found in many ministries and many churches. Larger percentage of Christian movies makers and actors in Nigeria are alumni of Mount Zion Institute. Four of the Six members of the Board of trustees of All Nigeria conference of Evangelical Drama (ANCEDRAM) are alumni of The Mount Zion Institute of Christian Drama; out of the Five past Presidents of ANCEDRAM, from 1996 till 2010, Four of them are members of the Mount Zion Institute Alumni Association.

About Us

The Mount Zion Faith Ministries International mission is to evangelize the world through the means of evangelical drama and movie productions. Mount Zion Television Productions supplies christian movies and television serials to interested TV stations and Cable and Satellite Channels. We are serving many TV stations in Nigeria, Europe, America and Africa.
